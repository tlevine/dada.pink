Application requirements and procedure

Admission and application

English proficiency

For MSc

• We require a Toefl (Ibt), Ielts or Cambridge (CPE or CAE) certificate. We do not accept any other certificates, letters from your university or personal statements on English proficiency. Please follow the link for the required scores.

• You need to provide (a copy of) your language certificate before the application deadline of 1 April 2014 (EU/EFTA nationals may provide the certificate until 1 July 2014).

Diploma’s / former education

For MSc

We are not able to assess your eligibility for admission on your diploma or university name alone. You need to provide us with a complete application for the selection committee to assess.

Recommendation letters

 

There is no specific format for the MSc recommendation letters. We request you to provide two, signed, recommendation letters; one from a professor or an assistant professor from the faculty from which you graduated and one from your employer, if you already have work experience. If you have not yet graduated or don’t have work experience, please provide two letters from professors or assistant professors from the faculty at which you are studying. Your recommenders should not send the letters directly to TU Delft; you need to include them in your application package. It is not necessary to provide the letters in a sealed envelope.

Application fee and fee payment

For MSc

• You can pay the application fee by credit card or by bank draft transfer. Payment details can be found through the link.
• Please note: if you pay by bank transfer: the accountholder name is TU Delft F and C; you can add the name of Mr. A.H. Dijkgraaf if necessary.

Scholarships

Scholarships and Awards

All complete applications which arrived before 1 November 2013 will be considered for the TU Delft Excellence Scholarships (DRI Scholarships, Justus & Louise van Effen Scholarships and Scholarships linked to the research themes Climate, Process Technology, Robotics and Transport). Scholarship recipients will be notified by mid March 2014.

All students admitted to faculties and programmes which offer TU Delft Faculty Scholarships and Industry Scholarships will be considered for these opportunities (also if the application has arrived after 1 November 2013). Scholarship recipients will be notified before 1 June 2014.

The following faculties do not offer any scholarships; Architecture, Mechanical - Maritime and Materials Engineering and Aerospace Engineering.

If you are granted a scholarship TU Delft will inform you by e-mail. Only those students who have been selected will be contacted regarding this scholarship.
